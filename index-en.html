The tax calculation depends firstly, on the power of the engine kW (1kW = 1,36 CV-DIN) and secondly, on the emission rate of the vehicle. For the calculation of the bonus/malus, the following data is taken into account:

  • 50 % bonus if CO2 <= 120 g/km
  • 50 % malus if CO2 > 200 g/km

For cars that were first registered before 01/01/2010, the emission rate isn't taken into account.
